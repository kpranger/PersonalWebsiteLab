How Things Were...

Initially starting as a part time copy center associate for OfficeMax / Office Depot, I worked my way through every position available in store until I achieved a store manager position. After 10 years at OfficeMax / Office Depot, I decided to change companies and started working at AT&T. Quickly working my way up from sales representative to assisstant sales manager, I was not happy without running my own store. Since there were not any opportunities with corporate without relocation, I gave myself a promotion to store manager by switching to an authorized retailer for AT&T.

How It's Going...

After working retail for 15 years, I decided to invest in myself and take a new career path to enter the tech industry. I happened to see a social media post from an aquaintance about their experience at Grand Circus and decided to look into what it was all about. Fast forward 3 months and I am now almost half way through my training and on a my way to the tech industry.
